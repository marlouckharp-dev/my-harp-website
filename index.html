About

Hi, I'm Marlou — a harpist based in France with over 30 years of experience playing everything from classical pieces to video game soundtracks. By day I'm a neurobiologist studying fruit fly brain development, by night I stream live harp music on Twitch and YouTube!

I'm currently working on my first album of original compositions, and releasing harp covers on all major streaming platforms. I also offer professional harp recording and transcription services.

When I'm not playing harp or doing science, you'll probably find me eating dark chocolate 🍫 or farming in Stardew Valley 🌱 — sometimes both at the same time!
